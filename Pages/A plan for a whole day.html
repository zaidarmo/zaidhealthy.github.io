A Plan for a whole day

       

People will always need a sample to see how a healthy living person lives. So this is a plan or a routine for a healthy living person for a whole day. Also this will be a plant on Saturday so there will be no work or school.

8:30 AM: Wake-up

8:45 AM: Eat a breakfast full of carbohydrates

9:30 AM: Get a walk around the neighborhood

10:15 AM: Go back home

10:30 AM: Morning exercises

11:30 AM: Dish of fruits and vegetables

2:30 PM: Eat a supper full of proteins

4:00 PM: Play a sport like basketball or football

5:30 PM: Eat a bar of chocolate

7:30 PM: Eat dinner with cheese

8:15 PM: Get a walk around the neighborhood

9:00 PM: Night exercises

10:00-10:30 PM: Sleep
